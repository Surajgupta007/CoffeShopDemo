Who We Are

Started in 2010, Brew & Bean was born out of a simple love for a great cup of coffee. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it's a culture, an economy, an art, and a science.

We work directly with farmers to ensure the highest quality and fair wages. Every bean is roasted in small batches to preserve its unique flavor profile.
